KNOWLEDGE BASE

Frequently asked questions

Everything about Korean servers & localization.

How do I connect to a Korean server?
When you launch Adopt Me! on Roblox, the game will automatically route you to the nearest server with the best ping. You can also manually select “Seoul Main” from the server region dropdown in the game’s server list.
Can I play with international friends on Korean servers?
Absolutely! Just invite your friends via Roblox join feature. Korean servers support cross-region invites while maintaining low latency for local players.
Will events and updates be available on Korean servers?
Yes, every seasonal event, update, and patch rolls out simultaneously across all official servers including KR-dedicated nodes.
How to change game language to Korean?
Set your Roblox account language to Korean in Settings → Language. Many in-game texts and UI will be displayed in Korean, and more translations are rolling out progressively.
